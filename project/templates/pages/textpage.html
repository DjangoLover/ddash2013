Why?

Number of internal services used by the company increases every day?

Then, maybe, one of the major problems encountered is authentication.

First, for each service, the employee has a separate login and password. Change your password if need be - quite invoice procedure.
Second, there is no opportunity to influence the complexity of the password in the third-party services (i.e. qwerty).
Third, to reinvent the wheel for registration / authorization each time
you develop a new internal service - rather extensive routine operation.

Setup

Signup, invite your teammates and use OpenID authentication in your services! Setup organization identity, ie http://trilan.singlepointhq.com/openid/xrds/ and that's is all!
